## Cancelable

Android alerts can optionally be marked as `cancelable`. Clicking outside the bounds of the alert will dismiss it without a callback.

## Three Buttons

iOS alerts can accommodate any number of buttons. Each button can optionally specify a style, which is one of `default`, `cancel` or `destructive`.
