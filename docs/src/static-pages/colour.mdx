## Introduction

Our colour palette is built around a bold lead colour, Sky Blue. It represents clarity and optimism, and above all embodies the sky of a perfect trip — year round. 

We are a multi-colour brand, and support Sky Blue with a wider palette of warm, natural colours that represent the rich hues found all over our planet — from bustling cities to untouched natural landscapes.

## Dark mode palette

The Dark Mode palette extends our regular palette. The colours in this palette should <strong>never ever</strong> be used in light mode.

<DarkModeColors />

## Dark mode dynamic colours

Dynamic colours are colour values which automatically adapt for light and dark modes. If you use these colours, the dark-mode variation will automatically be applied when a device&apos;s dark mode is enabled.
